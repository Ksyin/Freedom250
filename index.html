Our Mission

What is Freedom 250?

A U.S. Embassy Nairobi-led initiative empowering Kenya's next generation of innovators, leaders, and changemakers through experiential learning.

Innovation

Hands-on tech experiences including Drone Soccer, Robotics Challenges, and VR explorations at The Garage tent — where ideas become real.

Collaboration

Direct engagement with American brands, Embassy leadership, and industry mentors from companies shaping the global economy.

Civic Engagement

Democracy simulations, voting booths, and leadership workshops at The Townhall tent — making your voice count from day one.

Culture & Music

Live performances, art installations, and the "Garden of Heroes" mural experience celebrating the freedom that connects our nations.
